Image Gallery of Churches - Grandpa's Church on Andøya Island, Norway

These images are of the Dverberg Church, belongs to the Church of Norway (Lutheran denomination)

My grandfather grew up attending this church.

The church was first built in the early 1700's, and then after it was destroyed by lighting, it was rebuilt in 1843. This is an "octagonal church", the architecture of which is unique to Norway.

Beginining in the 1680s, this "octagonal" style replaced the stave church style that had been prevalent in Norway.

Winter view of the church. Torstein Raaby, the famous artic explorer, World War II resistance fighter, and Kon-Tiki expedition member, is buried in the church cemetery.
